Hydrometer

Objective

To carryout grain size analysis of soil by hydrometer analysis test


Apparatus used:

Hydrometer, Evaporating dish, Conical flask, Measuring cylinder, Weighing balance etc.

STEP  1 

 Take 50 gm of the air dry soil sample.

00.00 g

50.00 g

STEP    2 

 Treat the soil sample with Hydrogen peroxide to remove the organic matter

STEP   3 

 Add 100mL of sodium hexametaphosphate and stir well

STEP   4 

 The suspension which is made up to 1000 cc is shaked, hydrometer dipped in distilled water is inserted in sample.
